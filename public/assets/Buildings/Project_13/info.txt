<section id="overview">
    <h2>Project Overview</h2>
    <p>The Charming Villa in Agios Georgios is a stunning residential property located in the peaceful countryside of Chania, Crete. Surrounded by olive groves and rolling hills, this 400 sqm villa is designed to blend traditional Mediterranean architecture with modern comforts. It offers a perfect retreat for families and individuals seeking a serene yet luxurious living experience close to nature.</p>
</section>

<section id="design-concept">
    <h2>Design Concept</h2>
    <p>The villa’s design embraces the charm of Crete’s traditional architecture, featuring stone walls, tiled roofs, and wooden beams. Modern elements, such as expansive glass doors and minimalist interiors, are seamlessly integrated to create a harmonious living space. The warm tones and natural materials reflect the surrounding landscape, while the open-plan layout emphasizes light and space.</p>
    <p>Outdoor living is a key focus, with shaded terraces, a private pool, and landscaped gardens that connect residents to the beauty of the Cretan countryside. The use of sustainable materials and energy-efficient systems ensures a balance between luxury and environmental responsibility.</p>
</section>
