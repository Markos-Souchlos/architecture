<section id="design-concept">
    <h2>Design Concept</h2>
    <p>The Modern Townhouse in Heraklion embodies contemporary urban living while respecting the cultural and architectural heritage of Crete. Its design highlights clean lines, open spaces, and high-quality materials, with a neutral color palette that complements the city’s aesthetic. Large windows and sliding glass doors bring in natural light and create a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor spaces.</p>
    <p>The townhouse is designed with sustainability in mind, featuring energy-efficient systems and locally sourced materials. The rooftop terrace and private garden provide a balance between urban convenience and serene outdoor living.</p>
</section>
