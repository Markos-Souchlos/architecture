<section id="design-concept">
    <h2>Design Concept</h2>
    <p>The design of the Beachfront Villa in Platanias celebrates the beauty of its coastal surroundings while offering a modern and luxurious living environment. The villa features open layouts, expansive glass facades, and a neutral color palette inspired by the natural tones of sand and sea. Locally sourced materials such as stone and wood enhance its connection to the environment.</p>
    <p>Key design elements include large sliding glass doors that open to spacious terraces, infinity pools that appear to merge with the sea, and landscaped gardens that reflect the villa’s eco-conscious ethos. The result is a harmonious blend of sophistication and nature.</p>
</section>
