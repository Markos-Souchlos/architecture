    <h2>Project Overview</h2>
    <p>The Eco-Friendly Home in Sissi is a sustainable residential project nestled in the tranquil coastal village of Sissi, Lasithi. <section id="design-concept">
    <h2>Design Concept</h2>
    <p>The vision for the Eco-Friendly Home in Sissi was to create a sustainable residence that maximizes comfort while minimizing its environmental impact. Clean lines, soft neutral tones, and locally sourced materials define the home’s aesthetic. The use of large windows and glass doors ensures an abundance of natural light and seamless integration with the surrounding environment.</p>
    <p>Outdoor-indoor connectivity is prioritized with open spaces, terraces, and gardens that celebrate the beauty of the Mediterranean landscape. Sustainability is at the heart of the design, incorporating green building materials and energy-efficient systems throughout.</p>
</section>

<section id="sustainability-innovation">
    <h2>Sustainability & Innovation</h2>
    <p>This home incorporates cutting-edge sustainable features to promote energy efficiency and reduce environmental impact.</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Solar Energy:</strong> Solar panels provide clean, renewable energy for the home’s needs.</li>
        <li><strong>Rainwater Harvesting:</strong> A system collects and recycles rainwater for irrigation and domestic use.</li>
        <li><strong>Passive Cooling Design:</strong> Strategically placed windows and shading reduce the need for mechanical cooling systems.</li>
    </ul>
